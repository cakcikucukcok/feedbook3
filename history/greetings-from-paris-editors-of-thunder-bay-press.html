Paris is one of the most thrilling cities in the world. From the Eiffel Tower, presiding majestically over the city, to the Mona Lisa, smiling her mysterious smile at the Louvre, the delights of Paris are known throughout the world. Once you’ve seen them, you’ll never forget them  whether it’s in person or in Greetings from Paris!

From its history to its architecture to its people, Greetings from Paris explores everything about this unique city in stunning full-color images of its past and present. Tour the Champs-Elysees and imagine yourself beneath the Arc de Triomphe; browse the shops and cafes of Montparnasse and the Quartier Latin; stroll the banks of the river Seine  with Greetings from Paris you can do it all!
