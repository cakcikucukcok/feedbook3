From acclaimed teaching physician, improvement advisor, and entrepreneur, Dr. Medell Briggs, comes The Visionary Leader: 7 Solutions to Implement Successful Change in Modern Healthcare. This title is the blueprint to pioneering your field with confidence, consistency, and powerful transformations.
