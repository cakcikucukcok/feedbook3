So your dream is to succeed in business, to develop your inner entrepreneurial spirit by getting into income property. Today it seems that all you need is an idea and a cell phone, and you can get started. Don’t be fooled. There are a lot of people developing some great new ideas; you can be one of them, but it takes work and guidance.

If you have an idea, then stop talking about it; do it. Don’t waste another day. No one is going to do it for you. You’ve got to get up and go for it, and you need the expertise of someone who’s done it already. The book you are holding contains the accumulated knowledge and know-how of a 40-year veteran of the world of business and real estate. In Slumlord Millionaire, Spanky Macher introduces you to the world of income property, helps you discern if you’ve got what it takes, and walks you step by step through the ins and outs of the industry, to your goal of success.

 

What you will learn from this book

  1. How to make money.
  2. How to access the tools that can make you successful.
  3. Where and how to buy property and how to make the most of your investment to build a portfolio.
  4. What is important for a renter or a buyer; what renters and buyers look for.
  5. What to do with clutter.
  6. How to determine and make renovations that count, and how to estimate costs.
  7. How to write a blueprint of success for your own goals.
  8. When to buy and when to walk away; the art of negotiation; and how to determine the right price.
  9. How to build curb appeal and give the right impression to your buyers.
  10. Whether to flip or to rent.
  11. How to structure your business and assign yourself a role.
  12. How to plan your entrance and exit.
