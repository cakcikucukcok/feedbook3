This is a poetic work of PassionLovePain and Struggle. It is a culmination of my total evolutionary experience, throughout my years of incarceration within The Georgia State Prison System. Black Berries gives you the reader, an inside look at how a mans mind can produce some of its best effects even when placed under some of the harshest conditions of living. Black Berries has been a work in progress for over the last 7-8 years. Choices are what we must make daily. By making the right ones, within ourselves to grow; we all have the ability to bring forth our highest qualities. Through Black Berries contents, I sought to touch and communicate to the best parts of our souls, as a People. I have sought to inspire, strengthen and to encourage the reader. I trust in the fact that, all who choose to read Black Berries, there will not be one reader who will not be rewarded. Black Berries will nurture and satisfy your black soul. It will enlighten and encourage you to stay strong.love yourself, love each other and GROW.
