This is a poem collection that only goes from dark to darker to darkest while giving it to you straight. Many souls can relate, many also can't. But if you ever wondered how the strong emotions of Pain, Struggle, Sickness, Fear, Anger, Disappoint can be expressed just for the sake of being expression and just let it your them roam free. Then go ahead and take A TASTE OF REAL LIFE. Thirteen poems comprised of revelations and expresses exactly how it's like going through the dark issues in life from Social Hypocrisy, Mental Illness to Feminism. They specifically shed light on on Betrayal, Relationships, Insecurities, Substance abuse, Narcissism, Anxiety, Amnesia, Insomnia.
