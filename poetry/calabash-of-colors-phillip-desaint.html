The POEM generically is the most colorful literary facility for articulating ideas whether of philosophical discourse, romantic rhapsody, or socio-political protest a kaleidoscopic omnibus. Calabash of Colors is therefore a vigorously varied pot-pourri of poems within which scope of structures, from metaphor-dense verses to easy-rolling prose-poems.
