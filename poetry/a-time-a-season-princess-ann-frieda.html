A Time & A Season by Princess Ann Frieda is a collection of personal poems written at different times and places, talking about different seasons of life which come and go. They are an expression of what goes on throughout the calendar year rotating the four seasons, winter, spring, summer and fall. Some are short poems, while others are longer, expressing deep rooted feelings, conviction and interpretations of circumstances surrounding her. Also these are cycles of life rotating at different times, deaths, births, weddings, graduations. Other times are fixed like Dec 25th, Feb 14th 4th of July, New Years Eve.
