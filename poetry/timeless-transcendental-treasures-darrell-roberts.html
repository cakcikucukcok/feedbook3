Transcendental Treasures is written with an eternal vision of love in mind. A vison of a pure and true love where there is an understanding that we are not just these material bodies. We are eternal, full of knowledge and bliss that is our essential nature, as well as the nature of all living entities. We are all here and now, bonded, to this space ship called Earth on a voyage through time and space and we are all connected and have a story to share. One key note is that because we are eternal, our journey and story started prior to this life and will continue after we move on to the next state of being.
