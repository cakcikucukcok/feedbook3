This book takes the reader along with the author into cosmic odyssey to make them feel as inseparable part of the universe. Her voice proclaims - Universe as our home and temple, humanism our existence. The author believes that the ultimate essence and purpose of all religions is - Universal love, brotherhood and Global family - as the way of life. The author regrets for people constructing bee-hive like compartments in their hearts, minds with dividing walls. She pleads the people to relalise the truth that the noble cause of their presence in this Universe is for spreading, love, peace among the people of the world..She calls to abandon brutality and promote universal love, brotherhood, humanism the true religion. She pleads all to introspect more by heightening ones inner spirit. She believes that the discourses of all treasured religious scriptures and preachings of all preceptors is to accord guidance to human civilization for peaceful co-existence. She foresee the inhuman perversions in future generations and desires the need to mould the children with humane traits and inculcate the culture of peace to guard them from vicious cultures, and the author calls for every person to march as Harbinger of Peace.
