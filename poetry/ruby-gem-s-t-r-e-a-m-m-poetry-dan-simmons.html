HOPEFULLY THIS BOOK DELIEVERS A IMPORTANT MESSAGE . THERE IS A GREAT WOMEN BEHIND EVERY GOOD MAN. I WAS BLESSED YET FORTUNATE TO BE SURROUNDED BY NUMEROUS FEMALES,AND MALES GROWING UP IN LIFE.THIS MADE ALL CHALLENGES VERY CONQUERABLE .FROM MY WIFE(RUBY ODESSA SIMMONS) ,MY SISTERS,TO MY COUSINS,NOT TO DISMISS MY MOTHER,AND MY AUNTS. FRIENDS AS WELL EXTENDED ME GRACEFUL SUPPORT DURING THE ROUGH PERIODS OF LIFE.I HAVE TO ALSO EXTEND A THANKFUL BLESSING TO ALFONIA AND MORRIS,EDDIE,GERALD AND MANY OTHERS WHO SIDESTEPED THEIR PRIORITIES,IN ORDER TO ASSIST ME. I WOULD LIKE TO LET THEM KNOW I APPRECIATE EVERYTHING THEY DID FOR ME. I WOULD LIKE TO DEDICATE THIS BOOK TO THEM, I COULD NEVER TELL YOU HOW MUCH THIS GROUP OF PEOPLE HELPED ME IN LIFE(WE WILL ALWAYS LOVE THEM)THANKS FOR EVERYTHING. DAN EDWARD SIMMONS
