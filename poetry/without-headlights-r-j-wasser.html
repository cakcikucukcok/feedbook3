What Fresh Hell waits within the pages that are ahead of you? What wisdom can you draw from the experiences of a more than travelled explorer of the world?

This collection of poems is exactly that; the collection of wisdom, experiences and lessons learned by RJ Wasser over the course of the last decade which included heartbreak, multiple combat deployments and more insanity than most people see in a lifetime. Poetry and prose have often been used for cathartic reasons but this collection of writings is undoubtedly more of a confession to deeds desires and capers that most could barely imagine.

So what Hell does wait within these pages? Is there a peaceful serenity to the story or does the rabbit hole continue to fall farther and farther into an almost willful madness?
