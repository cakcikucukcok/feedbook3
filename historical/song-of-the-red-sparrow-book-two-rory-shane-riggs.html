So much has happened in the twenty years following the Civil War, the most shocking event to stun the country to date. As a young nation fights for survival and identity, its citizens seek their own purpose and understanding. In Prairie Gulch, a hidden town in Texas, what seems on the surface like the innocent arrival of four singers puts in motion a series of events that could alter history itself.

One of the young entertainers could be the key to unlocking a mystery a local myth discussed only in hushed tones that has captivated the attention of locals for years.

As the puzzle begins to come together, the townspeople may learn that some legends are better left unproven, and some secrets are better left buried.

Song of the Red Sparrow Book Two is the second installment in a trilogy that traces an epic American story. From the end of the Civil War to a small, isolated frontier town in Texas two decades later, guarded secrets are about to be revealed, lives changed, loves lost and history itself could be rewritten.
