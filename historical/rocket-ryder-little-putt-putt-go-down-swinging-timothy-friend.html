Violent, exciting noir fiction, laced with pitch black humour

When Rick Tanner and Scotty Crane get the news that their low-rent kiddie show has been cancelled they figure they’ve hit rock bottom, but when the director of their show is murdered, and they’re framed for the crime, they discover how far they still have to fall. The duo - one a terminally ill Korean war vet, the other a hopeless drunk - set out on a quest for blind justice that leaves a trail of bodies from Kansas City to the Ozarks. Their misguided mission brings them face to face with corrupt cops, gangsters, and an ex-criminal turned politician. As they discover the truth about their friend's death, and the untouchable nature of the guilty parties, Rick and Scotty realize there's no way out for them. 

It isn't a question of if they're going down, but how.
