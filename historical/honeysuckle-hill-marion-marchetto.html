"Merline and Daniel, now the new owners of a ramshackle house, get more than they bargained for when a resident Indian spirit tries to correct history. In this tale revealed through the houses droll sense of humor, Merline and Daniel learn about the history of their property and vow to set history straight. The Tavern at Honeysuckle Hill introduces a host of former inhabitants: the Pootatuck Indians, early European settlers, a naval family, artists, actors and gypsies. Based on the real-life Legend of Lillinonah, the story is set in Shelton, Connecticut where the Pootatucks once made their winter home.
