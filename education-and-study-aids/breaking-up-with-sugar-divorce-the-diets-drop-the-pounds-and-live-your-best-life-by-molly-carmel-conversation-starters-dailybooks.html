Breaking Up With Sugar: Divorce the Diets, Drop the Pounds, and Live Your Best Life by Molly Carmel: Conversation Starters

Breaking Up With Sugar: Divorce the Diets, Drop the Pounds, and Live Your Best Life is a 2019 book written by Molly Carmel. It is a book explaining that the solution to weight problems and too much food is breaking up with sugar. Being an eating disorder therapist, Molly Carmel was able to help thousands of people recreate their lives. She was able to assist them on how to defeat repetitive unsuccessful diets, obsessive overeating, and addiction with sugar. The book is a 66-day blueprint for going sugar-free.

Molly Carmel's Breaking Up With Sugar: Divorce the Diets, Drop the Pounds and Live Your Best Life was published on December 31, 2019. It became the #1 New Release in Amazon.
