Want to expel the images of children and babies in hospitals with broken bodies, or the body of a slain child or baby found in a hidden grave from your mind? Television news, radio, and newspapers repeatedly broadcast our police forces being thrown into harms way when called to intervene in a domestic violence. Sinful behavior means our hearts are not right with God. Thus, the constant stream of images of harmed and dead children will only cease through the complete surrender to God and the power of the Holy Spirit working in our lives.

The life choices you make determine the outcome of whether your children live in a family characterized by the harmful, destructive actions of abuse and violence, or grow up in a family where fathers and mothers parent in the power of Gods love in action.

I heartily endorse Mrs. Hummels books on parenting. After parenting four biological children and seven foster children with special needs, I can say that two of the secrets to effective parenting are the size of your parenting toolbox and the number of your resources. This book provides many resources as well as ideas to grow your toolbox.

Randy Feola, senior pastor, Grace Mountain Church, Colorado Springs.
