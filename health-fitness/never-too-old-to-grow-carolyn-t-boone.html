This book is meant to inspire, inform and enlighten you through scriptures from the Bible, Kings James Version, famous quotes, one liners and short poems. The focus of this book is on everything positive. Such as, Psalms 118:8 is the center verse in the Bible, which says, It is better to trust in the LORD than to put confidence in man." This verse says something significant about God. He had to be in the center of it and should be the center of our lives. There are notable quotes such as, "Peace demands the united efforts of all of us", by Haile Selassie, and one liners like, there is absolutely nothing wrong with me; God doesnt make mistakes.

This is a little book of wisdom. It could be used as a reference book. It is formatted in alphabetical order with an index for easy access. Good, quick and easy reading. A book that you would read over and over again.
