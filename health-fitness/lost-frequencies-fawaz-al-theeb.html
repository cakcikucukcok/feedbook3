It is a hard world out there, and it is even harder when you lack the experience and just try to face it all alone. You got to shape your life. The way you wanted to. Look at the moon, its lonely and it just cant stop shinning. And the reason I named the book Lost Frequencies is because it is normal to get lost at least once in your life. And remember, you only get lost to be found all over again. I am not going to lie to you. It is okay to get lost ,to be flawed, to be mistaken, to be human. Perfection does not exist. You are only pursuing the shadow of you. You are flawed, and you are perfect that way.
