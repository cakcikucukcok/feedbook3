Besides being self-employed and working out of her own home, which she inherited from her biological father, something else was going on in that house, and it had absolutely nothing to do with her beauty salon. Were strange things happening because of a longtime friend whom she allowed into her home? Just goes to show that you cannot trust people no more than you can yourself!
