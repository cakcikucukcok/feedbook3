IT’S TIME TO PUT A SMILE ON YOUR FACE

 

Am I Too Old For A Million Dollar Smile? offers straight forward advice to help adult patients gain a beautiful, dazzling smile. Whether you are considering cosmetic dentistry, trying to restore proper function to your mouth; concerned about finding the right dentist; or worried about cost, this book can be your personal guide. It represents the culmination of Dr. Behzad Nazari’s 18 years of experience in dentistry, as well as his collaboration with specialists with whom he’s been associated throughout his distinguished career.  

As we all know, a confident smile can be a ticket to success—even a richer life: a better job, more friendships, even some romance.  A brilliant smile instantly changes how you feel about yourself and how others see you.  So it is important for patients to know how to chose the right dentist; understand their treatment options; and why some of the procedure/recommendations are made by dentists.

Am I Too Old For A Million Dollar Smile? puts to rest the adult patient’s worry about getting his or her teeth checked, answers basic dental health questions, and explores advances in modern dentistry.

Dr. Nazari discusses why orthodontic treatment may be necessary before full-mouth reconstruction or cosmetic dentistry. He goes on to take the mystery out of:

•  “invisible” braces and traditional braces for adults

•  newest technologies (such as cone beam X-rays)

•  veneers

•  dentures

•  implants

•  and much, much more

 

So, it’s absolutely true when Dr. Nazari says,”You’re never too old for a million dollar smile!”
