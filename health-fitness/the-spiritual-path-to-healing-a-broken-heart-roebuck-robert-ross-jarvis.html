The Path to Healing a Broken Heart is a remarkable story that highlights the tragedy of divorce and the triumph of being single. The author, Robert Roebuck, discovers the reality of God’s presence along with his journey of despair and disappointment. His experiences were heartbreaking, but Robert rebounds, and says, “The cure for a broken heart begins with the repair of fragile, fractured relationships.” The Path to Healing a Broken Heart has so many nuggets of practical spiritual advice and wisdom that you will have to pause for a moment, put the book down to cry and laugh, then take a deep breath and begin reading again! Chapters 16, “Appreciating our Struggles,” Chapter 17, “How Did We Get Here!” Chapter 18, “Fixing a Broken Heart!” takes you into the heart and soul of the journey towards spiritual and emotional healing with a storyline that rivals any romantic adventure.

The Spiritual Path to Healing a Broken Heart teaches us to laugh at adverse circumstances and the stupid things that we did, not with disregard, but about God’s purpose. Despite the misfortunes he faced, the author finds a way to overcome the obstacles and challenges through four stages of spiritual growth.
