For high quality dementia care to be provided, professionals need to be both knowledgeable about dementia and skilled in the provision of care. This book is an introductory reference guide that will help students, professionals and practitioners develop their skills and expertise to better respond to the needs of people with dementia. It sets out information and advice on essential topics, research and evidence-based practice within dementia care in a clear, jargon-free, sensible way. Organised based on the Dementia Core Skills Education and Training Framework and the Higher Education Dementia Framework - the standard course structure for dementia education - this comprehensive title covers topics including dementia diagnosis, person-centred care and law, ethics and safeguarding. A new go-to book for the dementia curriculum, it is an invaluable tool for anyone wishing to improve their core skills and knowledge of dementia.
