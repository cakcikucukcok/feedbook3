Revenge of the Cannibal Chickens is a roller coaster of hilarity, as two chooks, Dougy and Dermot, hit the road in search of the true meaning of a chicken's existence. Driven to the egging of the century by a sadistic farmer, Dougy and Dermot steal his truck and take to the highway in search of the chicken kings! On their way, they have an uncomfortable encounter at the "Food Smart," a high-speed chase with the police, before coming to the understanding that a chicken's life is more than just something that goes well with potatoes and gravy. These two easy-going roosters who know nothing more than that going on within their coop, take a stand for chickens everywhere, going on a frenzy of feathered madness to exact revenge on those who barbarically use chickens for whatever purpose. They work their way up the pecking order to become a statement for chickens everywhere. Filled with laughs and life's challenges, people of all age groups can relate to the book, but especially boys ages eight to twelve. One of the classics!
