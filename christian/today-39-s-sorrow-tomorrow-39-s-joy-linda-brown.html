He was called as a child, a young boy. Called to reach the Indian people in the Southwest Territory of Oklahoma. This calling led Daniel Gentry to a Bible Seminary in Illinois where he met his future wife, Rachel. Together they studied and planned to move west. Others caught their vision and either joined them on the journey or joined them through prayer and financial support.

As they pulled away from Cedar Grove, telling those they loved goodbye, Preacher Clyde Carter prayed them on their way: “. . . and precious Father, we ask You to do whatever it takes to bring the people of the villages where Danny and Rachel will minister, to know You . . .”

But what do you do when God changes your plans? “Today’s Sorrow, Tomorrow’s Joy” takes you on the journey of Danny Gentry. Two words describe this young man: Surrender and Obedience. Through the unexpected events of his life . . . Danny remained faithful.
