WHAT WILL IT TAKE TO SILENCE YOU?

Tyris Bonds is Philadelphia's newest problem. He doesn't market his services, he doesn't have an office, and he doesn’t work for anyone, but it doesn't matter. His criminal record and years worth of street cred speak for themselves.

After serving time in prison, street thug—sometimes robber, sometimes drug dealer, or both—is on the loose. But instead of working his way to becoming a rich Philadelphian, he sets out to reclaim his spot and make his name ring even louder than it did in the past. With the help of some friend’s, he develops one of the biggest crime syndicates the city has ever seen.

Taking on this roll is no easy feat, and there are opposing forces that keep guns blazing and the death toll rising. There is always a hefty price to pay, and before everyone goes to jail, Tyris has to use his all of his weapons to be sure Nobody Knows Nobody when the chips fall.
