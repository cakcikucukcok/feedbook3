A Charming Seducer…

James Garrison is charming, friendly and mischievous—he’s also the pastor of Living Word Christian Church. And he’s found himself walking the path of personal darkness. He is spiritually lost, but doesn’t seem to care about how his personal actions affect others, especially the church. He’s searching for something… or someone, rather. James decides that his secretary and friend Nylah Grace Anderson is a perfect choice.

has set his sights on one,

Grace Anderson … has always held James with high regard. Knowing he could never be interested in her, she’s content with being his friend, while enjoying their working relationship. James surprises her by asking her out to dinner, it soon becomes clear, dinner is not all he desires.

 

How Far Will They Go?

Knowing she would have to compromise to be with James, Grace is torn. Is this what she wants? When they find themselves in a seductive, potentially gratifying situation, will they make the right choice? Or will Grace join James along his path of unrighteousness?

This book is the beginning of a tale involving the realization of personal truths, dealing with the consequences of our own actions and knowing that redemption is available for all.
