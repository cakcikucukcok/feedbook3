'Still Fighting' is an inspirational literary fiction about Joe whose parents went out of the village to a city to avoid being banished by the king as a result of his troublesome lifestyle.

Joe found a career in professional boxing and was popularly known as 'Still Fighting' because of his unprecedented display of enthusiasm and the chanting of 'Still Fighting' after either winning or losing a fight in the ring.

Joe soon became the toast of the media all over the world with increasing number of fans. He became a brand ambassador to a number of corporate firms.

While granting an interview on the day of his retirement from his boxing career, he discovered a previously hidden ability in him to become a motivational speaker and success coach by telling inspiring stories.

His stand against oppression, corruption, human right abuse, and perversion of justice prompted him to defend a helpless victim of a uniformed man and thus floated 'Still Fighting Foundation' as a platform to fight against gross misconduct in the society.
