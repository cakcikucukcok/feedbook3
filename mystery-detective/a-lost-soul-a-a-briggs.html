Ashlyn Ann Williams is a rebellious young lady. She is flirtatious and lavished with attention by men. She agonizes every day, but her parents ignore her pain. Her choice of poison is alcohol. Once she is forced to overcome the death of her best friend, the consummation of poison increased. After many irresponsible decisions; it compelled her to experience losing close friends, and gain enemies. Will Ashlyn defeat her demons or self-destruct?

Darnel Danny Collis is a charming man that is infatuated with control. He knows how to entice woman to fall for him. Everyone is unaware of his past. His skeletons are hidden deep inside his closet.

A Lost Soul is a suspenseful journey of pleasure, sex, violence, and many consequences. It is a tragedy that is dedicated to life experiences. It explores what the characters are impelled to overcome.
