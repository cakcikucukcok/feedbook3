Every night when she closes her eyes…she  sees him.

A new beginning…

Eight years ago, Trish Delancourte was abducted by a ferocious serial killer. Abused, tormented, and left for dead, she escaped within an inch of her life. Now, she’s trying to get her life back together. She’s moved to a new town. She’s graduated from college. Everything is exactly the way she wants it, until the first body shows up in the small town of Serenity, North Carolina. Just as the Black and White Strangler had once killed his victims. Only there’s one problem…he’s dead.

The return of the prodigal son…

                          For the last ten years, Cameron Randolph has been living life in the fast lane. But when an accident nearly takes his life, he’s forced back to the small town he grew up in and nothing is as it seems. Living in the house he grew up in with his four brothers once more, he’s faced with the ramifications of his decision to leave ten years ago and he meets Blake. A twelve-year-old boy with scars, and whose life is in danger. Not to mention the gorgeous woman who shows up on his doorstep.

The beginning of the end…

The moment Trish lays eyes on Cam she finds herself instantly attracted to him, but she can’t act on it. She’s meant to be alone. He made sure of that eight years ago, but when Cam saves her life she finds herself turning to him. Drawn into a web with a madman, she soon finds herself facing her darkest fears and Cam is facing demons of his own. For a dark secret is threatening to break free, threatening to change everything…forever
