Ex-CIA operative Kilkenny writes the book on surviving a post-apocalyptic event or fall of civilized society by becoming a professional Marauder! The book takes you through the how tos, the been-theres and done thats, while telling true personal stories from the authors own experiences in lawless societies and escapades in the War on Drugs, Revolution in Latin America, the Blood Diamond Conflict and more. Doomsday Marauders outlines everything you need to know about: - handguns - rifles - shotguns - body armor - night vision - IEDs and more to succeed in the next life. It describes, using fictionalized stories, how to take down farms, ranches and even large survivor colonies in order to survive, even thrive after the fall of civilized society. Read how Kilkenny, in his humorously witty fashion describes: - Escaping a jail cell in Mexico - Taking out potential kidnappers - Tracking down and killing rogue Contras in Nicaragua - A stare-down with the Cartels most vicious killers All told with an eye toward instructing you, the reader, how to survive situations that seem hopeless. Theres always a way! Dont miss one word of this witty, intelligent and informative read!
