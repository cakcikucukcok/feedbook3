An abused child of Cuban refugees, a drug-dealing teenager, an addict, and an entrepreneur. Jose Del Cueto emerged from the depths of adversity and now struggles with trauma-induced learning disabilities. His journey has taught him the humility, mindfulness, and compassion it takes to be the loving family man he is today.

"Medical research on trauma-induced ADHD predicts a poor outcome in adult life, yet Joe’s story is of hope for  millions of people who endured childhood trauma and the learning disabilities or substance abuse derived therefrom.” — Michael J. Marchese, M.D.

“When you sense that something is wrong with you but don’t know what it is, an answer can really help you lead a better life by putting things in perspective. I am grateful to have found my answer.” —Joe Del Cueto
