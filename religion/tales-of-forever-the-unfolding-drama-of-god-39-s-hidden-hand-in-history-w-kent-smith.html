EXPERIENCE FIRSTHAND a fantastic journey of discovery beyond the veil of time and space—a journey that will transform both you and your understanding of God's control and faithfulness...

To anyone who cares to investigate such matters, the history of our world reveals evidence of a remarkable drama of God’s control and faithfulness, involving many courageous people, exotic places, and milestone events. A sweeping tapestry of the ages, it is a drama played out on the stage of world history for all to see, yet ironically, it is one that, by God’s marvelous design, is quite literally hidden in plain sight—sublimely obvious to some, completely veiled to others.

This book, Tales of Forever, which is actually two books presented in one volume, sets out to describe this subtle yet pervasive drama, and it does do so against the backdrop of five things, five sacred things—The Ark of the Covenant, The Spear of Destiny, The Shroud of Turin, The Great Pyramid of Giza, and The Septuagint Bible.

Along the way, we encounter several more things, among them, an ancient prophecy—a promise of "days," which precisely foretold the Advent of Christ and which bears a striking similarity to the messianic chronology found in the book quoted most often by the writers of The New Testament. In addition, we meet the man who was commissioned to tell the Tales of Forever, a man set apart from all others who was said to have talked with God, face to face, having a conversation that took place not in any earthly realm but at the very pinnacle of Heaven itself.

All these elements, then—a man and his heavenly conversation, a promise of "days," an ark, a spear, a shroud, a pyramid, and a book, that is to say, everything needed to demonstrate God’s control and faithfulness—have for the first time been forged into a single storyline, to tell the Tales of Forever: The Unfolding Drama of God’s Hidden Hand in History.
