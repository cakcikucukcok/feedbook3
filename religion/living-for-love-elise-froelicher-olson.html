To love others well is not easy, yet what a joy to be well loved! Explore what God has revealed about his interactive plan of hope, discovering how we are enabled to take down our walls and love others. This easy-to-read book assures us that the God of the universe has not forgotten us, but loves us deeply and has a compassionate plan that is unfolding even now. Respond to Gods call to love and protect as you prepare and go forward, making a difference in the battle between good and evil that is raging in our world. Find truth and true love, one step at a time, as we open up and respond to the love of God. Find the help you need to persevere through the frustrations and hardships of life. Be inspired by true stories. Look forward to a time when perfect love will be present for all of eternity. God is preparing us now through his interactive plan that improves our relationships and our lives. Discussion questions help us explore Gods plan for our own lives and encourage meaningful friendships. Your life matters! We can make a positive difference in our world with Gods help, one person and one nation at a time.
