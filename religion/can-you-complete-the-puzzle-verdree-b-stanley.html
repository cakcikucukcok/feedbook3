Unwittingly Hope

In his third volume, Dr. Verdree B. Stanley considers the ways in which life situations are not always manifested from left to right and from bottom upwards. Sometimes, circumstances require a person to start at the end and work their way to the beginning, or start from the top and work their way down to the bottom. Sometimes, acquiring a goal is determined by the point of reference. A straight line is not always the shortest distance and a circle does not always connect. Each of the seventy-one crossword puzzles has its own unique design and can enhance growth in an individuals life.
