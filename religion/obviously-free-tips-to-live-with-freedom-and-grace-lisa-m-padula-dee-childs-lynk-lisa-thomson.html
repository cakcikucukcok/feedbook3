 The Christian life is supposed to be like a race…a journey we run freely. Often our own behavior deprives us of our freedom and makes the race seem strenuous and futile. Lisa set out to pick 14 simple, “obvious” tips that, when lived and practiced daily, would be the impetus (kick-start, so to speak) to a Grace- and Freedom-filled life. She is convinced that real, genuine change in our lives comes from purposefully striving to live with the full understanding of how much God loves us! How much GRACE he has extended to us…

Think about it! Meditate on this fact! Let it really sink in…

He gives us freedom in His sight…and we can give freedom to others!

He gives us grace…and we can extend grace to others!

Combined with illustrations from her long-time friend, these short, to-the-point, “rubber meets the road” lessons will inspire you to pursue an “obviously free” life!
