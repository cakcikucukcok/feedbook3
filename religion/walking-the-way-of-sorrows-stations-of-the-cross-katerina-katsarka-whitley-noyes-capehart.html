On Good Friday and throughout Lent, Christian congregations around the world walk the Stations of the Cross in commemoration of Jesus' walk to Calvary. This book is a fresh resource for those wanting to explore the meaning of the Stations more deeply. Features powerful blockcuts, monologues from the viewpoint of someone at each Station, biblical references, and brief liturgies. Excellent for individual devotion as well as for groups.
