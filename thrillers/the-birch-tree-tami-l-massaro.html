Three best friends, Molly Barr, Judy Woods, and Lee Kellerman, are happy to be leaving their indistinguishable high school years behind and are looking forward to what the future holds for them. Little do they know that before the night is over, their lives will be changed forever.

Held up at a nearby construction site, the girls are late to the graduation celebration being held along the river. The party is already in full swing when they arrive and alcohol is freely being passed around. Not accustomed to drinking, Molly soon gets sick and heads back to the car, leaving her friends behind.
