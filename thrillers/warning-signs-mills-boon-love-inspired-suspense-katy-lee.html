When a drug smuggling ring rocks a small coastal town, the DEA sends Agent Owen Matthews to shut it down. A single father with a deaf son, Owen’s instincts tell him that the town’s number one suspect—the high school’s new principal—doesn’t fit the profile.

Miriam Hunter hoped to shrug off the stigma of her hearing impairment when she returned to Stepping Stones, Maine. But her recurring nightmares drudge up old memories that could prove her innocence—and uncover the truth behind a decades old murder. Yet Owen’s help may not be enough when someone decides to keep Miriam silenced—permanently.
