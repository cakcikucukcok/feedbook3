Two worldslife and death Ashley, once a curator becomes a teacher due to an unexpected scenario and leaves Hawaii to take up her first job as a teacher set in a village in Tanzania. In her search for understanding, she grapples with lifes big questions. Can there be life after death? Can the dead return to quench the thirst of desire? Bewildered but keen for comprehension, Ashley embarks on a quest for understanding her new environment. Before she knows it, she is pulled into a confusing and mystical world of slavery, poaching, pirates, and a vicious blood feud. Her journey has taken a new route as her friends (Dominic Thurston and Edwin Brown) and students (Jude Gilliam and Margarita Flinghead) share her curiosityand six mischievous spirits keep her on her toes. John Mosaic and Barney Peacock, the enigmatic creatures whose magical abilities intrigue Ashley, have their own motivations to get involved in the mortal world. The colorful feathers and mosaic elements flourish the intense drama and is lit up with magical dust of hope, love, and friendship. It gets captivating as Ashley encounters a new love interest. Can Ashley untangle the mystery before it is too late? Will universe manifest her dream? Can she trust her own instincts? This tale brings one to a new expedition of travel, love, magic, humor, thrill, and suspense. Are you prepared for the lesson? The Bell has rung!
