Shattered Emotions is a thrilling, tear-jerker novel that will leave you spellbound from beginning to end as it introduces a host of interesting characters that takes the reader on a journey of love, suspense, action, mystery and drama. A multi storied novel that introduces and captures the very essence of ordinary peoples lives as they experience the good and the bad of just living.

Joe Wilson is a young married man coming home from the war to reunite with his wife, but she was unaware that hes arriving today. William Davis is a man that wants to undo something hes done, but he cant. Linda Wilson has a secret and shes afraid to tell what it is. Something very personal is revealed inside a miniature safe. Grace Jones has an unexpected visitor during the night.

This powerful, captivating, heart-breaking novel will soften your heart and grip your emotions as various families deal with lifes good, not so good, shattered dreams and broken hearts. It depicts how bad or inappropriate decisions can easily become life-threatening.

Shattered Emotions is a must read for those that like action, suspense, love, drama, adventure and mystery.
