Rick has been lonely ever since his wife divorced him and living alone on his farm is getting him down. One day, while in town, he discovers that a young man is looking for a room to rent. Rick decides to get a lodger but he isn't quite prepared for Fabio. Fabio works as a hairdresser in town and the two men are at opposite ends of the scale. However, it doesn't take long for Fabio's charm to win Rick over and soon he is asking himself some probing questions. Fabio has always said that he doesn't find Rick attractive and the big farmer wants to know why. Will he be able to handle the truth? Part romance and part erotica this short story of just 7 000 words is perfect for when you are short of time but still want some sizzling passion in your life.
