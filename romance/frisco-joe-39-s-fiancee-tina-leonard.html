He almost shooed them away--then he looked into single mom Annabelle Turnberry's beautiful blue eyes, and "Go away" became "Stay...."

Oh, my! The eleven hunky examples of pure testosterone intimidated the once-burned, twice-shy Annabelle, especially the tall, dark and glowering Frisco.
