Diana Davis is not sure of anything anymore. After her recent divorce, move to a new town, and empty nest, she finds that she must start over at forty. With her life turned upside-down and depression nipping at her heels, she mysteriously receives a gift from her dearly departed Grandmother. 

Comforted by the reminder of her grandmother, she reluctantly steps out into her new world and makes the acquaintance of an unusual group of southern church ladies, “The Goddess Tribe”. Gradually, life begins to make sense to Diana, as she and her world start to blossom, incorporating the lessons of the Goddess Tribe. As Diana embarks on a spiritual journey, she heals her wounds, discovers love, and finds that there truly is magic in life.

This is a heart-warming romance in the visionary fiction genre which outlilnes a woman's spiritual journey from depression and sadness to a new life.
