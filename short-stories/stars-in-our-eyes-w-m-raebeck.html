"Stars in Our Eyes" is a fresh and savory story collection. Fiction can't compare to this kind of truth—a grab-bag of irony and spice of life. You'll find yourself in Rome, Tribeca, Baja, Hollywood, jail, Camelot, Kansas, and the hospital. You'll do plenty of star-gazing, follow some dreams and watch others crumble. The subject matter ranges from fabulous high heels to movie auditions to lousy tenants to the guy at the airport—all soulfully original and light when you need them to be. These twenty vignettes are an unusual blend of casual reminiscence and deep, poignant reflection. But they inexplicably adhere to a theme—spirits, coincidence, dreams, and the tough truths we’d rather run from. You'll be curled up with this book right through the last story.
