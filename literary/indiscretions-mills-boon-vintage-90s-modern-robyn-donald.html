Bride's Bay Resort

Compelled, charmed… and compromised! Mariel loved Bride's Bay Resort, its Sea Island location, its friendly staff. She'd jumped at the offer to translate there again - but soon wished she hadn't. For one thing, working for a security-conscious delegation was no job for a woman with a past.

For another, there was Nicholas Leigh, the most commanding, charismatic man Mariel had ever met and - for her - the most dangerous! From the start a feral and magnetic attraction crackled between them. An affair with a delegate would be indiscreet enough. If Nicholas discovered her carefully covered past, too, it would destroy both their lives.

For his sake - for hers, too - Mariel had to get out of this man's life… . But first she had to break the spell that bound them!
