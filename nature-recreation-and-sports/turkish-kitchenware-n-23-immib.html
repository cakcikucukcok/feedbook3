We have met with you at the Ambiente Fair, one of the great meeting points in the

kitchenware and housewares sector. We are extremely excited and honored to attend the

Ambiente 2017 Fair for the 22nd time together with our 72 companies that are

Istanbul Minerals and Metals Exporters’ Association (?MM?B) members and delegates

from Turkish national participation.

Turkish houseware and kitchenware continues to expand globally with new fairs and

organizations. Istanbul Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Metals Exporters’ Association (?DDM?B)

organizes the first national participation to Spring Fair with the participation of 10

companies in Birmingham, on 5-9 February, 2017. The Turkish pavilion will be held at Hall

9, and will also be the sponsor of the Seasons’ Lounge at Spring Fair. Should you attend

Spring Fair, please come and visit the Turkish Housewares Pavilion as well.

Meanwhile, we would like to give you an update on the exciting work that the

Turkish housewares and kitchenware sector has been undertaking.

We organized the first overseas activity under the Development of International

Competition (UR-GE) project for Turkish housewares sector in Indonesia and Vietnam.

21 companies from Turkish Housewares UR-GE Project participated in this organization

which took place on March 20-27, 2016. The project has been organized by Istanbul

Chemicals and Chemical Products Exporters’ Association (?KM?B) and Turkish Housewares

Manufacturers Association(EVS?D).

As for the second stop in our efforts to enhance the sector’s competitiveness, we chose

France. 29 companies from Turkish Housewares UR-GE Project attended this organization.

Last but not least we travelled to both Peru and Mexico. 19 companies from the UR-GE

joined this organization.
