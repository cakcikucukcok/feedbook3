A reliving of earth and pain is a saga set in the distant future, after the great 'destroying,' which is said to have ravaged the earth over two hundred and sixty million years ago. The remnants of the human species have been drastically regressed into another brutal iron age, D.U.S.Ts., dormant nanotechnology permeates the very air they breathe, and the order of the VICE WOMB, a matriarchal religious organization is bent on controlling the masses.

For years, Danso, a user of Direct Utilization of Small Technologies, has been a disabled slave unable to connect and use such devices, serving within the order's sanctuary, waiting for the opportunity to kill his wife Nissa, a narcissistic priestess who had his pupil and true-love Gho’a murdered. Nissa also attempted to have Danso murdered in order to rise in power. When news of Nissa’s arrival to the sanctuary is made known, so is Danso’s purpose, and the governing priestesses, the feminarchs, seek to terminate him. He must find a way to reconnect with the D.U.S.Ts. in order to survive long enough to intercept and kill Nissa before the order finishes what she started.
