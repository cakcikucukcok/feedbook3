Forty years ago, a drifting world -- a rogue planet -- passed through the solar system. Using scavenged alien technology, a single ship was launched from Earth, in the hopes of contacting whoever or whatever might be controlling the unknown intruder. Officially, neither the planet nor the mission ever existed. Unofficially, the ship and its pilot were both lost without a trace, but even that was a lie. There were reports.
