There is a little town in Central New York that is rocking with New Life simply because of a 30 year high school class reunion.The whole country is focused on this event because St.Micheals' Catholic Academy class of 1977 has produced more famous people than any other school in the country.There is a world known architect billionaire, who was the poorest boy in his class. He builds a magnificent monumental setting for this reunion which changes the whole town.

Although he has built many all around the world this one is special in that it will be the place where he will be reunited with the Love of his life.There is also an Acadamy Award actress, a financier, an astronaut,a nobleman,a senator,a scientist and a world famous missionaty. Each life appears to be a transformation of simple to sublime. Then something dramatic happens to show that everything is not really as it appears on the surface.. They are all forced to see what is truly real and face their connectedness in a way they could never have expected.
