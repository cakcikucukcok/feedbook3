Sometimes reality mimics fiction.

It’s an election year, and Americans are ready to choose the man, or woman, to lead the country. Never before has the debate been louder. Can a businessman with no political experience really become President?

The Warrior’s Soul: A New Birth of Freedom is set in an alternate future where representative democracy in America has collapsed due to lack of compromise and stalemate. The territory of the United States has been absorbed into a large regional trading block run by an all-powerful CEO. The government entity runs like a business, acts like a business, and is not hampered by the moral or constitutional boundaries of traditional democratic government. There is one man at the top, and what he says goes.

Does stability and prosperity mean more to us than the sometimes messy, sometimes dysfunctional democratic process?

The Warrior’s Soul: A New Birth of Freedom is more than just satire on current events. It deftly mixes in elements of fantasy — with a hero who has literally stepped right out of Greek mythology — and of course action and adventure. Romance too. Follow star-crossed lovers across the pages where good battles evil and a lost people try to find their way back.

Can the brave few prevail against the many? Can blood and hope give us all — as Mr. Lincoln once said — “a new birth of freedom?”
