Set in a feudal land of loosely affiliated "Houses," a young woman of privilege is thrust into a position of leadership for which she has no experience. Her people are at war, attacked by marauding barbarians. Her parents are dead, her house burned. The odds are overwhelmingly against her; not all the Houses support her. Her only loyal friend appears to be her lifelong companion, Baldor, a telepathic ice bear. She must first survive and then unite the great Houses in one last desperate attempt to defeat their enemy. To make things worse, she may be falling in love with one of the enemy.
