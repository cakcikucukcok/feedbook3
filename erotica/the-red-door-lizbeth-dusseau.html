A bizarre journey awaits Lily Matisse beyond the red door. A simple flower seller at a street corner stand, shes lured through this ancient gateway by a mysterious lover, Ravel. A primitive goddess in an orgy on the beach, a bawdy tavern wench serving a brutal master, a slut on trial for treason, and a princess doomed to be the pawn of an evil sorceress are just some of the astonishing women she becomes on the other side of Ravel's Red Door.
