Lauren is a lonely housewife, living with a husband who's rarely home because of his work. When he is home, he's too tired to do anything with her and their dry spell in their bedroom is reaching new heights. After her husband leaves for work, Lauren gets ready to run some errands but her car stalls. This isn't the first time this has happened. In fact, she had just gotten her husband to take it to his friend's garage shop to get it fixed for the same exact problem. While she has never met her husband's friend, she decides to take the car in to his garage to get it looked at again. Once she reaches the garage, she asks for a loaner and one of the workers offers his expensive SUV. Lauren visits the grocery but when she returns, she finds a huge scratch running along the side of the car. She worriedly takes the SUV back and tries to explain but the angry black owner of the SUV is having none of it. He offers her a choice, pay for the repairs with cash or fulfill his desire. Lauren is stunned by the choices but because of her long dry spell, she decides for the second option. As more of the black garage shop workers surround her, she feels a lust within her burst into flames. Does she have what it takes to the fulfill the desires of three big black men at the garage shop?
