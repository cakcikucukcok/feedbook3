Holly, at 23, is just graduating from university and after increasing problems with her boyfriend she breaks up with him. A series of happy events in her life is capped with the chance meeting with Ricky, who quickly introduces her to sexual experiences that she had never encountered in her previously sheltered life.

Holly brings him back to her rented apartment for a very long night of sex, after which Ricky shows her what it is like to make love where there is the possibility of being seen. Holly becomes more adventurous with her love-making as each encounter shows her willing to learn and put her own spin on their time together.

During this chapter in her life Holly gets her degree, lands her first job and is awarded several prizes from people as rewards for the way she helps other people. Her sex scenes are depicted in detail, with multi-page build-ups that will leave you wanting to read them again and again so you can cry out in delight with her.
