Nova’s search for truth and justice continues. Her old boss at the jewelry shop unexpectedly gets in touch, has he found a new clue? When she returns home she finds her apartment broken into, and Simon is standing outside the open door. Her apartment is turned upside-down and her computer has been stolen. Could it be the sexy Simon who broke in and stole her evidence? This is the third installment of the series about Nova. Salt and Pepper is a short story of the genre erotic noir – suspense fiction with streaks of erotica.
