Meet Cody Michaels and BethAnn Wilson. Cody falls in love with her in their freshman year of high school. It takes three years of trials and tribulations for them to finally get together and when it happens they quickly develop a passionate, intimate relationship.

Follow Cody and BethAnn as their relationship intensifies and they try to live their lives and work their plans for the future.

From one extraordinary event to the next see what Vince has in store for the two lovers!
