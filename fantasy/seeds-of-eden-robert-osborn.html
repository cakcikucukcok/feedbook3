Earth

The task of convincing the Earths leaders the Edenites are there to help is gaining ground with the President and the Department of Defense. Brad Bowen, former FBI tactical team leader is fully onboard and in control of the Earth team. Recruiting for team members is bearing fruit and setting up a joint base at Area 51 is moving forward. Will their efforts be enough to stem the tide of the encroaching crime syndicates, terror cells and anti-government groups and still prevent the abuse of the planets resources from triggering the planets destruction?

Bara

The Okee under threat, their population in demise. The Princess Alexis told by a Houn and a Baar, her husband to be was from Earth, a man named Mathew. As per prophecy, only he will do. Logan, the Android warrior the only one who can arrange for Mathew to come to Bara and the only one standing between the Okee and the Pache

Pache

The Pache at war on three planets. Having built up their war machine, they plan to overthrow the Okee government and enslave its people, but first they must kill the Warrior Logan and capture the two Princesses.

Mathew

His original mission to save Earth from self destruction. Now asked by the Council of Planets to travel to Bara and save a kingdom by force and marriage. Onboard the Edenite Warship Black Falcon 8 as it travels towards Bara, he must arrive before the Pache can take his would be bride and force her to marry one of their own. The Baar and the Houn anxiously awaiting his arrival.
