My story begins in Medieval Britain, in the year AD 603, with the Holy Sword Excalibur being returned to me as promised by my grandson, the good King Arthur. I am The Lady of the Lake, and I bring you this prophesy: your life will be changed forever once you find the truth hidden within these pages.

I'm an engineer; I don't believe in ghosts and spirits. Yet since that night in Spain, when Mum appeared to me in a vision, something has awakened inside me and I'm restless to find answers. So here I am in Scotland, in search of buried treasure with my beloved knight by my side.

I sense that Mum is still present, reminding me that her message is urgent. Yet despite everything that has happened, it still hasn't occurred to me that I’ve been sent on a quest to find King Arthur’s Holy Grail. As the Seven Seals are broken and their secrets are slowly revealed, I discover that this life is the latest chapter in an epic story of romance, tragedy… and quantum physics.

Based on a true story.
