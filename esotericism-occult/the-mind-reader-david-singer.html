What would you do if you could read minds? Dr Finker had no idea, but he's about to find out!

The retired dentist wasn't looking for these amazing abilities. He was simply looking for a cure for the hand tremor that drove him out of his profession.

After a Brazilian healer cures his ailment, Finker discovers that the ritual has also given him powerful psychic abilities.

He wants to use them to advance peace and justice, but sinister operators might force him into using his powers to cause chaos and catastrophe.

David Singer was inspired by his own experience in the Dominican Republic. When his father took ill, a local man came to visit and performed a miraculous healing ritual. It awakened Singer's curiosity in non-traditional medicine. Singer spent over forty years as a dentist.
