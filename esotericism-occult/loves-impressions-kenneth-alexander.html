Kenneth Alexander masterfully has crafted Loves Impressions. A Spiritual Poetry Collection of 12 divinely inspired poems. Each poem transcends you to a higher place while allowing you to grow your love and humility for yourself and others as you remain earthbound. Take a journey through heavens gate, discover that the sun shines in all of us, and experience love like you never have before. Cuddle up with your loved one and recite a poem in a tender moment, to touch their heart or simply to say, I love you. If you are a believer or seeker in law of attraction, enlightenment and spiritual growth, these poems will uplift you and inspire you to love yourself, God, and others to new heights.
