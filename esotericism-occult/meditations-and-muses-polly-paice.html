I have been guided to work mainly with the earth vibrations, the things that go unseen in our busy daily lives.

I find that being in nature, laying on your back watching the clouds, watching the bees on a lavender bush, or really listening to birdsong can raise my vibrations to allow me to enter a meditative state.

I have used nature in my guided meditations, to allow you to capture these things. To raise your vibrations and allow your own inner selves to take over; taking you to a higher level of consciousness.

I have also included a few of my poems and muses which are of a spiritual nature.

I hope you enjoy my small contribution to the light work that is taking place all around the world.
