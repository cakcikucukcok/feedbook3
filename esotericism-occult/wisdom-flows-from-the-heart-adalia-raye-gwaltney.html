Wisdom Flows from the Heart: Inspiration for the Heart and Soul is about establishing a relationship with God through His Son Jesus Christ and discovering the love of God. You will experience that God is the potter and we are the clay, and if God cleaned me up and set my life back on track, He can do the same for you. We were born to fulfill our purpose in life, and we need direction to make our vision become a reality!
