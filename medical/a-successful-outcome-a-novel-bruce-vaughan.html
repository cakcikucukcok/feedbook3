“A Successful Outcome will bring you to tears, awaken your spirit and inspire you to appreciate this thing we call life with all its potential.  I highly recommend this book for anyone dealing with a cancer diagnosis.” – Jonathan Landsman, Host of NaturalHealth365.com

Set in Quenten, a fictional suburb of Boston Massachusetts, this story is fiction, however, based on today’s reality and depictive of the canvas that is the USA. Gripping and controversial, the story follows the lives, and in one case death, of three people: two diagnosed with cancer. Cancer has become the modern day scourge, affecting the lives of countless people both directly and indirectly.

Phil Armstrong, an attorney, loses his first wife after her five-year struggle with cancer. Years later his new fiancé, Jennifer, an investigative print journalist, faces the same fate. Together Phil and Jennifer seek alternatives to the standard approach to cancer treatment and recovery. Not only do they discover many natural approaches to healing, but their journey is thwarted by the inhuman conspiracy to prevent access to the healthcare they need most. The two work to uncover the truth, while others try to bury it.
