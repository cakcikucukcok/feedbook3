As stated by Dr. Mitchell Elkiss, Neurologist:  "… the book … is written by somebody who was brain injured and as such it gives me, as a medical professional, a glimpse into how it feels to be in that situation and what it takes to be functional again.  The book includes some interesting insights, such as the fact that waking brain injured people from their sleep is painful for them, the importance of emotional work for recovery, and the way to recover normal vision after brain trauma.

The book is organized in a practical format.  It can be read in detail or used as a reference, because it is written with the clarity of a scientist and the insight of a compassionate healer.  I wholeheartedly recommend this book."

A highly successful engineer and inventor, with a Ph.D. in building computers with circuits modeling the brain function, Susana Stoica was born with the gift of being able to work with the human energy field.  After going through two concussions on the same day, she discovered that her gift of working with brain trauma was not as common as she previously thought.

The book “Healing My Brain, Reclaiming My Life” was born out of Susana Stoica’s  own need to find a way to recover from the double concussion.  After unsuccessfully looking for reliable recovery tools, Susana Stoica used her previous experience as an energy healer to define her own recovery program using the most effective alternative modalities and defining some new ones.  In “Healing My Brain, Reclaiming My Life” Susana Stoica shares her recovery program and also the tools she used to more accurately diagnose the extent of her brain injuries and to limit the effect of trauma on the brain.

The book is intended for people with brain trauma and their caretakers, as well as doctors and nurses working with brain trauma.
